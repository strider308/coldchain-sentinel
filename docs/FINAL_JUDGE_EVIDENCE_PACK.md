# Final Judge Evidence Pack

Phase 39 provides a judge-facing index at `/judge-pack`. It connects the Command Center, algorithm evidence, behavior predictions, inspection guidance, six case narratives, the fault atlas, scale profiles, and explicit limitations.

## Routes

- `/judge-pack` and `/judge-pack.json`
- `/judge-pack/demo-script.json`
- `/judge-pack/technical-proof.json`
- `/judge-pack/claims-boundary.json`

The pack reports committed synthetic STBL evidence: 171,000 training windows, 38 fault prototypes, 19 weighted features, and both neural research and distilled runtime metrics. The technical proof describes the stdlib runtime and its lack of GPU, PyTorch, notebook, database, or external-service requirements.

## Boundary

All evidence is synthetic-only and advisory-only. Deterministic rules remain authoritative, human review is required, and the app cannot take operational disposition or outbound messaging actions. The pack makes no external validation, regulated approval, customer integration, or deployment performance claim.
